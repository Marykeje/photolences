Who we are

PhotoSceneLenses Africa is a non profit project under Unique Motion Media Konsult. It is a joint word from Photo Scene and Lenses which are keywords in Still and motion pictures . This non profit project started in 2017 in Ibadan when the founder and Convener started training young people who were interested in filmmaking for free. The idea of this project is to equip, inspire and empower Africans who have passion and talents for filmmaking/ acting for self sustainability, personal transformation and societal development. Over the years , we have trained and equipped hundreds of Nigerian youths for free.

Our Vision and Mission

This program is a rare bridge between young passionate African youths , people who don’t have the means to learn this art (Filmmaking & Acting) and those who are successful and have made a legendary impact ; where they get trained and get an opportunity to be mentored by the best in the industry. This event is a totally free mission. We do this as charity to help others grow and become great for themselves and their communities.

Meet Our Founder

Akinnayajo Babatunde also known as Mr Tunez Filmmaker is a Film & TV Director / Producer for over 10 years. He has produced Films , Television Projects & commercials both home and abroad. He is a coach, teacher and mentor to many who have passed through PSL Africa. He holds certifications in Humanities, Filmmaking and strategic Management. His drive is to bless and equip millions across the world. PSL Africa is a tool to reach the world; One person at a time.
